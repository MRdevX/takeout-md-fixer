About Takeout Metadata Fixer

When you get your photos from Google Takeout, there’s a small .json file next to each picture or video. It holds the date, time, and location. This app copies that info into the files themselves (using a helper called ExifTool) so when you import them somewhere else, everything shows up the way you’d expect.

I made this after my own messy Takeout import. Hope it helps you too.
